RULES OF CONDUCT

  1. Respect your fellow partiers: There is no tolerance for violence or disrespect between attendees of The Roman Rave.
  2. Respect between staff members: Staff members of the organization are also expected to respect one another.

  4. Follow Staff Instructions: attendees must adhere to all instructions given by the staff. This includes any directives related to the removal of attendees from the premises.
